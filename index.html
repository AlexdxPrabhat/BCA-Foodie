Briyani

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chiken
  • Masala
  • Vegetables

Recipe

Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, and salt. Cook basmati rice with whole spices until 70% done. Sauté onions, cook marinated chicken until tender. Layer rice over chicken, drizzle saffron milk, and cook on low heat (dum) for 20–25 minutes. Serve with raita or salad.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom

Recipe

Grind cashews into a fine powder. In a pan, add sugar and water, cook until it forms a sticky syrup. Add the cashew powder, cardamom, and ghee, cook until it thickens. Transfer the mixture onto a greased plate, flatten it, and let it cool. Cut into pieces and serve.
